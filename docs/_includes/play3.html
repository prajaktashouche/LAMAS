Description:

This play starts differently from the other two. Alice is given an A♠ and a 3♥, Bob is given a 2♠ and a A♥. Charlie is given a 3♠ and a 2♥.
First, Alice places down her A♠ and truthfully announces her action. Neither Bob nor Charlie can doubt, as neither of them have both Aces, so they accept. But now Bob knows that Charlie has at least one non-Ace card, and viceversa.
Next, Bob must place a 2. He places down his 2♠, and truthfully announces it . Neither Alice nor Bob can doubt, as neither has both 2s in their hand.

Alice must now place down an Ace. But she doesn't have any, and places down her 3♠, falsely announcing that she has placed down an Ace. But Bob knows this is a lie, as he actually has the only Ace in his hand. He doubts Alice's move, and thus she loses.
