Simulation

We have 3 players: Alice, Bob and Charlie. The deck consists of A♠ 2♠ 3♠ A♥ 2♥ 3♥. At the beginning of the game, each player is given two cards at random from the deck. The deal is shown on the right side of the graph.

To see the state of each player's hand, hover over each node in the Kripke model. The real world is w0. The colored arrows show accessiblity relations for Alice, Bob and Charlie. Each turn, each player decides to place a card or pass (see "Announcement" below). Cards are placed face down.
